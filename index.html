Archana Verma

Archana recently received her Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering, pursuing her passion for physical chemistry research. Her graduate research focuses on molecular modeling of doped organic semiconductors. She received her Bachelor’s degree in Chemical Engineering from Stanford University in 2021. She is fiercely passionate about supporting women in STEM, advocating for accessible mental health care, and combating climate change. In her free time, you can find her dancing, reading, cooking, playing badminton, making art, volunteering, shopping, taking care of her plants, or spending time with friends.
